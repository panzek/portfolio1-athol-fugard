Short Bio

Athol Fugard is arguably South Africa's greatest and most influential playwright. Fugard was born an Irish Roman Catholic father and an Afrikaner mother on 11 June 1932 in Middelburgh, a small village in the Karroo district in South Africa.

Read more

"As fascinated as I was by words on paper, it was matched by my fascination with words in people's mouths. The spoken word. And that is the world of theatre."
- Athol Fugard

Fugard has published over fifty books including plays and novels.
